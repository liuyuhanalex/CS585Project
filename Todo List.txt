1.Extract frames from videos
2.Resize all the frames
3.Subtract background from frames
4.Finish the Upper pattern of ALMD and lower pattern of ALMD
5.Training all the data 
6.Random forest for classification
7.Test our result

Main Algorithm(Feature Extraction)

Input:RDD[Video_Name N,Video_Data D]
Output:RDD[Video_Name,Feature_Vector V]
for all(N,D) in RDD[N,D] parallel do
	flatMap stage:
		BI = Background_Image
		for i = 1 to NumberOfFrames-1
			a = D[i]
			b = D[i+1]
			Ba = apply BackgroundSubstraction(a,BI)
			Bb = apply BackgroundSubstraction(b,BI)
			V[i] = apply Upper_ALMD(Ba,Bb)
			Q[i] = apply Lower_ALMD(Ba,Bb)
		end for
	add(N,V,Q) to result RDD
end for
